Why I built this

The Stock Portfolio Diversification Analyzer is a web-based tool designed to help investors understand their true sector allocation and identify hidden concentration risks. I created this project after realizing that most investors believe they are diversified, while in reality, their investments are heavily concentrated in a few sectors. Existing platforms show stock-level data but fail to provide clear, actionable insights at the portfolio level. This tool bridges that gap by calculating actual exposure based on investment value and highlighting overexposed and underexposed sectors. It also suggests how users can rebalance their portfolio to align with their target allocation. The goal was to build a simple yet powerful system that turns raw investment data into meaningful decisions.

What this tool does

Everything you need to understand and rebalance your portfolio — in one place.

🎯

Target allocation

Set exactly how much of your portfolio you want in each sector. The tool enforces a strict 100% total so there are no gaps.

📂

Zerodha import

Upload your holdings directly from Zerodha's Excel export. No manual data entry needed — the tool reads it automatically.

📊

Exposure analysis

See exactly how much of your portfolio is in each sector — compared against your own target. Colour coded for instant clarity.

⚖️

Rebalancing suggestions

Not just percentages — the tool tells you the exact rupee amount to buy or sell in each sector to get back on target.

📈

Visual charts

Bar chart comparing target vs actual, and a pie chart showing your full portfolio split by invested value.

📄

PDF export

Download your full analysis report as a PDF — including charts — to save or share with your advisor.

🔒

Private and secure

Every account is password protected. Your portfolio data is only visible to you — no one else can see it.

📅

Session history

Every analysis is saved. Come back anytime to view past reports and track how your exposure has changed over time.
